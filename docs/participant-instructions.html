Participant Instructions

You will use the study app to check multiple rooms. The app will tell you what to do and when to move. Follow the prompts exactly. Move naturally. If anything goes wrong, tap Report Mistake. You may stop at any time.

Open Checklist Back Home

Before You Start

  • Make sure the phone is charged.
  • Make sure the connected sniffer is attached if the app requires it.
  • Open the study app.
  • Choose the method assigned for this session: AR + Button, QR Code, or NFC Tag.
  • Complete any room setup shown by the app.
  • Do not enter rooms before the app asks you to.

Start Detection

  • After setup, tap Start Detection.
  • This starts the guided session.
  • This also starts network capture through the connected sniffer.
  • You do not need to start the sniffer separately.
  • From this point, follow only the app prompts.

AR + Button Method

  1. Confirm the room list or map shown by the app.
  2. Stand outside the room shown on the screen.
  3. Wait for the app prompt.
  4. Tap Start Action.
  5. Enter the room normally.
  6. Walk naturally along the instructed path.
  7. Exit the room.
  8. Tap Action Finished after you are outside.
  9. Wait outside until the app gives the next prompt.

What the app records

The app records the time between Start Action and Action Finished as the action period for that room.

QR Code Method

Setup

  1. Enter the number of rooms.
  2. Place or select one QR code near each room entrance.
  3. Scan the QR code.
  4. Name the room, such as Bedroom, Kitchen, Living Room, Office, or Bathroom.
  5. Confirm the room-to-code mapping.
  6. Repeat for every room.
  7. Review the room table before starting.

Detection

  1. When the app names a room, stand outside that room.
  2. Scan that room's QR code before entering.
  3. Enter the room normally.
  4. Walk naturally along the instructed path.
  5. Exit the room.
  6. Scan the same QR code again after exiting.
  7. Wait for the next prompt.

Action signal

The app marks that room's action signal as 1 between the two scans. All other room signals stay 0.

NFC Tag Method

Setup

  1. Enter the number of rooms.
  2. Place or select one NFC tag near each room entrance.
  3. Tap the NFC tag with the phone.
  4. Name the room.
  5. Confirm the room-to-tag mapping.
  6. Repeat for every room.
  7. Review the room table before starting.

Detection

  1. When the app names a room, stand outside that room.
  2. Tap that room's NFC tag before entering.
  3. Enter the room normally.
  4. Walk naturally along the instructed path.
  5. Exit the room.
  6. Tap the same NFC tag again after exiting.
  7. Wait for the next prompt.

Action signal

The app marks that room's action signal as 1 between the two taps. All other room signals stay 0.

During Waiting Periods

  • Stay outside the room unless the app asks you to enter.
  • Do not perform extra room actions during idle periods.
  • Keep the phone with you.
  • Wait until the app gives the next instruction.

If Something Goes Wrong

Tap Report Mistake if:

  • You enter the wrong room.
  • You scan or tap the wrong tag.
  • You forget to scan or tap before entering.
  • You forget to scan or tap after exiting.
  • The QR scan fails.
  • The NFC tap fails.
  • The app loses AR tracking.
  • You move before the prompt.
  • You are not sure what happened.

After you tap Report Mistake, the app will restart the affected step.

Privacy and Safety

  • The app records action timing and Wi-Fi metadata.
  • The protocol does not require audio or video recording unless separately stated.
  • Device identifiers should be pseudonymized before analysis.
  • Do not use the protocol in an unsafe situation.
  • You may stop at any time.
