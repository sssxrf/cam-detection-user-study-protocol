# Participant Instructions

Follow the app prompts exactly. Move naturally and report mistakes when they happen. You may stop at any time.

## AR Condition

1. Stand outside the room shown in the app.
2. Tap **Start Action**.
3. Enter the room naturally.
4. Walk along the instructed path.
5. Exit the room.
6. Tap **Action Finished**.
7. Wait for the next prompt.

## QR Condition

1. During setup, match each room to one QR code.
2. During detection, scan the room QR code before entering.
3. Enter, walk naturally, and exit.
4. Scan the same QR code again after exiting.
5. Wait for the next prompt.

## NFC Condition

1. During setup, match each room to one NFC tag.
2. During detection, tap the room NFC tag before entering.
3. Enter, walk naturally, and exit.
4. Tap the same NFC tag again after exiting.
5. Wait for the next prompt.

## Reporting Mistakes

Use **Report Mistake** if any of these happen:

- You enter the wrong room.
- You scan or tap the wrong tag.
- You forget to scan or tap before entering.
- You forget to scan or tap after exiting.
- A QR scan fails.
- An NFC tap fails.
- You enter too early.
- You exit late.

The app will log the mistake and restart that action step.

## Privacy

The study should not collect audio or video unless the researcher has separately explained it and received consent. Technical logs should use random identifiers instead of names, exact addresses, or real device identifiers.
